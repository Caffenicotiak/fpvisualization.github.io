Fluorescent protein properties   

Each fluorescent protein begins plotted with excitation wavelength on the x-axis and emission wavelength on the y-axis. The color is set based on its emission wavelength, and fades to gray as the brightness (product of exctinction coefficient and quantum yield) decreases. Mouseover each circle to see info on that protein. You can zoom using the mouse scroll wheel and pan by clicking and dragging. Use the filter sliders at the top right to select a subset of fluorescent proteins based on certain criteria. Use the X and Y axis toggle boxes to change what is plotted on each axis.
